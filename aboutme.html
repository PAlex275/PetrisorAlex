How I found this great world of internet technologies?

I used to sit at the computer more and more and begin to do more complex things like reinstalling the Windows, write CDs, things like that, but I chose to follow the music way, and attend a music school.
I started to be better and better at playing piano, many rewards earned, and contests won. In 4th grade, I participated in the national instrumental interpretation olympiad, obtaining the special prize of the jury. With the team, we participated in the contest First Tech Challenge and managing to obtain the 7th position out of 130 teams.
With one of the team members, I was involved in an experience change with the American School of Paris.
As you see, I'm a person who wants to be at the top, doesn't matter the context or circumstance. I want to be always professional and trying to improve.
